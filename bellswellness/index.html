Medical Disclaimer

Important information before using the app

General information only

Bells Wellness ("the App") provides general wellness information related to Bell's Palsy recovery. The information provided in this App is for educational and informational purposes only and is not intended as, and shall not be understood or construed as, medical advice.

Not a substitute for professional care

The App is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician, neurologist, or other qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding Bell's Palsy or any other medical condition.

AI and emergency limitations

The AI chat feature provides informational responses based on general Bell's Palsy knowledge. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice. AI-generated responses may contain errors or inaccuracies. If you experience severe symptoms, sudden changes in your condition, difficulty breathing, or any medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

Use at your own discretion

Recovery from Bell's Palsy varies significantly between individuals. By using this App, you acknowledge that Bells Wellness shall not be liable for damages arising from your use of or reliance on the information provided. You assume full responsibility for your health decisions.
