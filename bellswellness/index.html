Medical Disclaimer

Read before using the app

General information only

Bell's Wellness provides general wellness information related to Bell's palsy recovery. It is intended for educational purposes only and should not be treated as medical advice.

Not a substitute for professional care

The app is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always speak with a physician, neurologist, or other qualified healthcare professional about your condition or treatment plan.

AI and emergency limitations

The AI chat feature provides informational responses only and may contain errors. It should not be used for medical advice or emergencies. If you experience severe symptoms, sudden changes, difficulty breathing, or any medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

Your decisions matter

Recovery varies from person to person. Use the app as a supportive tool and make treatment decisions with qualified healthcare professionals.
