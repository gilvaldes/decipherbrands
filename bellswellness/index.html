Medical Disclaimer

Important use limitations

The text below mirrors the Bell's Wellness in-app medical disclaimer.

General Information Only

Bells Wellness ("the App") provides general wellness information related to Bell's Palsy recovery. The information provided in this App is for educational and informational purposes only and is not intended as, and shall not be understood or construed as, medical advice.

Not a Substitute for Professional Care

The App is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician, neurologist, or other qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding Bell's Palsy or any other medical condition.

No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Use of this App does not create a doctor-patient relationship between you and Bells Wellness or any of its affiliates. The App does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment recommendations, or medical opinions.

Individual Results Vary

Recovery from Bell's Palsy varies significantly between individuals. The timelines, exercises, supplement information, and general guidance provided in this App are based on general research and user experiences, and may not apply to your specific medical situation.

Supplement and Medication Information

Information about supplements and medications is provided for informational purposes only. Supplements can interact with prescription medications. Never start, stop, or change any medication or supplement regimen without consulting your healthcare provider.

AI Chat Feature

The AI chat feature provides informational responses based on general Bell's Palsy knowledge. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice. AI-generated responses may contain errors or inaccuracies. Always verify information with your healthcare provider.

Emergency Situations

If you experience severe symptoms, sudden changes in your condition, difficulty breathing, or any medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately. Do not rely on this App for emergency medical situations.

Limitation of Liability

By using this App, you acknowledge that Bells Wellness shall not be liable for any damages arising from your use of or reliance on the information provided. You assume full responsibility for your health decisions.
