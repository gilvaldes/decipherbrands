Bells Wellness Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Bells Wellness ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • When you sign in with Apple ID, we receive your name and email address (if you choose to share it)
  • Apple may provide a private relay email address instead of your actual email

Health & Recovery Data:

  • Recovery start date
  • Affected side of face
  • Daily check-in data (pain levels, mood, exercise/supplement completion)
  • Journal entries
  • Recovery photos you choose to take

User Preferences:

  • Notification settings
  • App preferences

Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data:

  • App usage patterns (which features you use)
  • Crash reports and performance data

Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (for app functionality only)

2. How We Store Your Data

Local Storage (On Your Device)

The following data is stored ONLY on your device and never uploaded to our servers:

  • Recovery photos
  • Daily check-in history
  • Journal entries
  • Health/mood data

This means your most sensitive information never leaves your device. If you delete the app, this data is permanently deleted.

Service Processing

Authentication and purchase status are handled through Apple services.
For AI chat responses, your question is sent to the Bells Wellness API and may be processed by OpenAI to generate an answer.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized recovery guidance based on your start date
  • Track your recovery progress over time
  • Send limited weekly recommendation-change notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Generate anonymized, aggregate statistics (no personal data)

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your health data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to the App

4. Third-Party Services

Apple Sign-In

We use Apple Sign-In for authentication. Apple's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

AI Chat Feature

When you use the AI chat feature:

  • Your questions are sent to our API server
  • Questions may be processed by OpenAI's API to generate responses
  • We do not store your chat history on our servers beyond the current session
  • Chat conversations are not linked to your personal identity in our logs

Analytics (Optional)

We may use privacy-focused analytics to understand app usage patterns. These services:

  • Do not collect personal identifiers
  • Do not track you across apps or websites
  • Comply with Apple's App Tracking Transparency requirements

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers: With vendors who help us operate the App (e.g., cloud hosting), under strict confidentiality agreements

Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request

Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Bells Wellness, our users, or others

Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Apple Sign-In
  • Local storage of sensitive health data on your device
  • Regular security reviews

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Portability

You can view all your data within the App at any time.

Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through Settings > Delete All Data. This permanently removes:

  • All check-in history
  • All recovery photos
  • All journal entries
  • Your profile information

Notifications

You can enable or disable notifications in the App settings or through your device's Settings app.

Account Deletion

To delete your account entirely, contact us at bellswellness@decipherbrands.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Users

The App is operated from the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification for significant changes

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

To exercise these rights, contact us at bellswellness@decipherbrands.com.
